# Multiple Positions Available

## Xing Xudong Laboratory
Beijing Institute of Genomics (BIG), Chinese Academy of Sciences
The Xing Xudong Laboratory at the Beijing Institute of Genomics (BIG), Chinese Academy of Sciences, is recruiting outstanding researchers to join our interdisciplinary team at the interface of artificial intelligence, multi-omics biology, and precision medicine.
Our lab focuses on developing next-generation computational frameworks and AI-driven models to understand cellular heterogeneity, disease progression, and sex-specific biological mechanisms using large-scale single-cell and multi-omics datasets.
We welcome highly motivated candidates who are eager to work in a collaborative, research-intensive environment.
 
## Open Positions
### Postdoctoral Fellows / Research Scientists
#### Area 1: Artificial Intelligence & Algorithm Development
Qualifications
-	Ph.D. (completed or near completion) in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Automation, Electronic Engineering, or related fields.
-	Strong expertise in deep learning, model development, and algorithm optimization.
-	Demonstrated experience in scientific programming and large-scale data analysis.
-	Excellent written and spoken English.
Preferred
-	Track record of first-author publications in reputable international journals or conferences.
-	Experience applying AI methods to biomedical or multi-omics data.
 
#### Area 2: Bioinformatics & Precision Medicine
Qualifications
-	Ph.D. (completed or near completion) in Bioinformatics, Genomics, Computational Biology, Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Medicine, or related disciplines.
-	Strong background in bioinformatics analysis and high-dimensional data integration.
-	Experience working with single-cell or multi-omics datasets is highly desirable.
-	Excellent written and spoken English.
Preferred
•	Demonstrated publication record in peer-reviewed journals.
•	Experience in translational research or AI-driven biomedical modeling.
 

### Graduate Students (Ph.D. / Master’s)
We also welcome applications from highly motivated graduate students with strong quantitative or biomedical backgrounds. Students will receive close mentorship and training in interdisciplinary research, including computational modeling, multi-omics analysis, and AI-based tool development.
Outstanding students will be supported in applying for competitive scholarships and research grants. Graduates from the lab pursue careers in academia, research institutes, hospitals, and industry, both in China and internationally.
 
## Responsibilities
-	Lead or contribute to independent research projects in AI-driven biomedical research.
-	Develop computational methods for multi-omics data integration and modeling.
-	Collaborate with experimental and clinical researchers.
-	Mentor junior students and contribute to grant applications and manuscript preparation.
 
## Compensation and Career Development
-	Competitive salary commensurate with experience and qualifications.
-	Eligibility to apply for national and institutional postdoctoral funding programs.
-	Opportunities for career advancement, including potential transition to long-term or faculty-track positions for exceptional candidates.
-	Access to state-of-the-art computational and multi-omics resources.
 
## Application
Please send the following materials to xingxd@big.ac.cn with the subject line:
“Application – Xing Xudong Lab – [Position Title]”
-	Curriculum Vitae (including publications and contact information for references)
-	A brief research statement (1–2 pages) describing previous work and future research interests
Review of applications will begin immediately and continue until the positions are filled.
We are committed to fostering an inclusive and collaborative research environment and encourage applicants from diverse backgrounds to apply.
